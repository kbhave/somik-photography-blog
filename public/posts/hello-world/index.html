Hello, new blog

Moving from Squarespace to Hugo — why and what's next.
July 3, 2026 · 1 min · Somik

After a couple of years on Squarespace, I’ve moved this blog to Hugo hosted on GitHub Pages. Faster, cheaper (free), and I own the content in plain Markdown files rather than locked inside someone else’s platform.

The migration took an afternoon. Everything that was on the Squarespace blog is being moved over gradually.

A few things coming soon:

  • Notes from recent shoots
  • Behind-the-scenes from client sessions
  • The Dark Sky Guide — a comprehensive astrophotography location guide for the USA I’ve been building

Stay tuned.
